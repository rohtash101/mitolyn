Privacy Policy Of Mitolyn

At Mitolyn, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to ensuring a secure, safe, and enjoyable online experience for all visitors and members. You can explore the valuable resources and opportunities available online, knowing that your privacy is protected.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information provided by visitors and members using our services. "Visitors" are those who browse our website without creating an account, while "Members" are those who subscribe to or purchase our products. Please take a moment to review this policy to understand how we handle and safeguard your personal data.

Information We Collect and How It Is Used

We collect various types of information to improve your experience and tailor our services to meet your needs. In most cases, your personal data will not be shared with third parties unless required by law or with your explicit consent.

Registration Information

When you register for our services, we may ask for personal details such as your name, address, phone number, payment information (e.g., credit card number), and device data. This allows us to manage your account, process payments securely, and offer personalized services. We do not share this information unless explicitly stated or in specific cases, such as during promotional partnerships where basic data may be shared to assess the success of a promotion.

Partners and Sponsors

We may collaborate with third-party companies to offer additional products or services. If your data is collected by a partner, we will inform you in advance, allowing you to decide whether to engage with their services. We may share aggregated, non-personal information with our partners, but your personal information will always remain confidential.

Online Shopping

When you make a purchase or subscribe to services (e.g., newsletters), we may request personal details such as your name, billing address, and payment information. For gift orders, we may ask for the recipient’s contact details. This information is solely used for processing transactions and will not be shared with third parties, except when necessary to complete your purchase.

Advertisements

We may display ads based on information we collect, such as browsing habits and demographic data. Advertisers will not have access to your personal details and will only view non-personalized data.

Email Inquiries

If you contact us by email, we will use your email address solely to respond to your inquiry. Your email address will not be shared with third parties or used for any other purposes.

Customer Surveys

Occasionally, we may conduct surveys to improve our services. Participation is voluntary, and all responses will remain confidential. We may aggregate survey results and share general insights with third parties, but individual responses will always stay private.

Promotions

We may run contests or sweepstakes, and in some cases, we may share the winner's details with third parties. If you enter a promotion, your personal data may be shared with the event organizer to notify you if you win.

Special Cases

We may disclose your personal information if required by law, to protect our company, or to investigate violations of our terms of service. Any such disclosure will occur only when necessary and appropriate.

California Residents' Privacy Rights

In accordance with California’s privacy laws, this policy details the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and who we may share it with. California residents have specific rights related to their personal data under applicable privacy laws.

By using this website, you consent to the collection and use of your data as outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy rights, please contact us for more information.
